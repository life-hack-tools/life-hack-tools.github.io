About

Say goodbye to
forgotten purchases.

StockHome is an app that makes it easy to manage your household inventory. Register food, daily essentials, medicine, and more by category. When stock runs low, items are automatically added to your shopping list. Share your inventory with family or roommates using an invite code, with real-time sync.
