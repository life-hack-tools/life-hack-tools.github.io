StockHome

Household inventory management app

About

StockHome makes it easy to manage your household inventory. Track food, daily supplies, medicine, and more by category. When stock runs low, items are automatically added to your shopping list. Share your household with family or housemates and keep everyone in sync.

Features

  • Category-based inventory (Food, Household, Healthcare, Other)
  • Low-stock alerts and auto-generated shopping list
  • Household sharing via invite code
  • 3 languages (Japanese, English, Vietnamese)
  • Offline support with automatic sync
