TodoBox

About

TodoBox is a clean and simple task management app. Focus on what matters without the clutter of complex project management tools. Just add tasks, check them off, and move on.

Features

  • Quick task creation
  • Simple check-off interface
  • Clean, distraction-free design
  • Lightweight and fast
